# Privacy Policy for FitAI / Pulse AI

Last updated: May 2026

## Data We Collect
- Account information (name, email)
- Health profile (age, weight, height, fitness goals, health conditions)
- Workout logs and progress
- Nutrition logs
- AI chat conversations

## How We Use Data
- Personalize your fitness and nutrition coaching
- Generate AI-powered recommendations
- Track your progress over time
- Improve the app's AI models

## Data Protection
- All data is encrypted in transit and at rest
- Health data is protected with Row-Level Security in Supabase
- We never sell your data
- AI conversations are processed by Groq and not used for training

## Your Rights
- Access your data anytime
- Delete your account and all associated data
- Export your data on request
